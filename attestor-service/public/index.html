Vouchsafe

Private, stake-backed proof of solvency for RWA issuers & FAsset agents on Flare (Coston2)

1 · Issuer / Agent — prove solvency private

Figures go into the TEE; only a signed attestation + FDC reserve proof reach the chain. Takes ~2 min (FDC round).

2 · Third party — verify no numbers

Paste an attestation id to see "solvent at T" with the input hash — never the figures.

3 · Fraud demo — slash accountable

A malicious attestor lies (insolvent). The fraud is proven on-chain and the stake is slashed. Takes ~2 min.
